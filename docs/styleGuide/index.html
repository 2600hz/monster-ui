Colors

Monster UI is a robust interface that displays very large quantites of information with variying levels of functionality. Due to the vast levels of hierarchy needed, a larger, more versatile color scheme is required to help with categorization. Included in the base color scheme are the main app color (Monster Blue), three tertiary colors (Emerald, Custard, and Candy Red), and nine neutral shades, which are broken down into three dark (Charcoal), three mid (Steel), and three light (Smoke) variations.
